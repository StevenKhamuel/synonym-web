Hello

Definition

an expression or gesture of greeting - used interjectionally in greeting, in answering the telephone, or to express surprise

Word Comparison

  • Hello is a very neutral form of greeting, acceptable in almost all contexts
  • Greetings tends to be more formal and is less common
  • Hi is a more informal greeting, but is still acceptable in most contexts
  • Howdy is fairly informal, and mostly used in the southern United States
  • What's up is a very informal greeting
